To backup your Cisco Network Building Mediator:
  • Click Backup to create a new archive file.
  • Download and store the archive file on your local machine.

To restore your Cisco Network Building Mediator from an archive:
  • Click the Browse button and select the archive file you wish to restore from.
  • Click the Restore button, which will upload and install your previous backup.
